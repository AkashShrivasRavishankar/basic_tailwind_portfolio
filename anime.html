Top 3 animes according to me as of now

Here are some of my favourite animes currently

Hunter x Hunter

A show which appears to be a kids show at the beginning turns dark and thrilling which impressed me.

Grand Blue Dreaming

This one is for the boys. A show that inspires you to live your life with glee.

Tokyo Revengers

Initially, I assumed that gang-related storylines would be uninteresting. However, this series impressed me by seamlessly combining elements of romance, time travel, and close male friendships.
